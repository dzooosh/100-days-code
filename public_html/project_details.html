United For Little Lights
250 Smiles for Idimu

We are thrilled to introduce our inaugural project, United for Little Lights, under the United Friendships initiative. On December 18, 2024, we will gather in Idimu, Lagos State, with the goal of supporting 100 babies aged 0-5. Through this project, we aim to provide essential resources such as financial aid, toys, baby food, books, and clothes to these children and their families. This initiative is our heartfelt effort to bring joy, comfort, and hope to the youngest members of our community, ensuring they have the tools and support they need for a brighter future.

Donate Now

Together, through United for Little Lights, we are not just offering aid; we are igniting hope and creating lasting change in the lives of these children. With your support, we can turn this vision into reality and ensure that every baby we touch grows up with the love, care, and opportunities they deserve. Let’s come together to light up their futures—one child at a time.

  • Date:18th December, 2024
  • Location:Idimu, Lagos State
  • Age and Gender:0-5 (male and female)

  • Minimum of 250 babies
