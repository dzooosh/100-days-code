About Us

United Friendships is a community-driven charity dedicated to empowering children, senior & young men through support, care, and opportunities, we aim to improve their well-being and foster lasting connections. Together, we build a stronger, more compassionate future for those who need it most.

  • Children First:
    • A Path to Success

    Guiding Voices: Our Founders & Mission Patrons

    Mrs Fadeke Atiku is the visionary founder of United Friendships, a charitable organization dedicated to supporting vulnerable groups in underserved communities. With a passion for community service and empowerment, she has directed their efforts toward improving the lives of babies, boys, young & senior men in the society. Born and raised in Enugu state, Nigeria, Fadeke developed a deep sense of social responsibility from an early age. Driven by a desire to give back, she earned a Bachelor of Science in Nursing followed by a Master of Science in Advanced Clinical Practice at the Anglia Ruskin University, where she gained valuable knowledge in community development, leadership, and social impact. This academic foundation, combined with hands-on experience in volunteer work and advocacy, laid the groundwork for the creation of United Friendships. Under her leadership, United Friendships has and will successfully launch multiple initiatives that will provide the much-needed assistance to hundreds of individuals, In addition to their work with United Friendships, she has collaborated with various local and international organizations to expand the reach of their charitable efforts. Her commitment to transparency, accountability, and long-term sustainability has earned the trust of donors, volunteers, and community partners alike. Fadeke’s vision for the future of United Friendships is to continue expanding its programs, reaching even more individuals in need, and creating sustainable projects that will empower generations to come. When not leading united friendships she enjoys traveling, mentorship speaking and reading which further enrich her perspective on life and the value of giving back
