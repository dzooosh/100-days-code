About Us

United Friendships is a response to the urgent need to provide aid & support to children, young and senior males in Nigeria, with the aim of empowering their growth and creating opportunities for learning and development through educational, financial, healthcare and social support founded by Fadeke Atiku Adeola, UF is dedicated to transforming the lives of children, young and senior males in Nigeria. Our mission is to empower these individuals by providing them with the support necessary to reach their full potential. We achieve this through a multifaceted approach that includes financial aid, comprehensive welfare programs, educational support, skill acquisition training, and access to healthcare. Through our targeted mentorship and awareness initiatives, we aim to create lasting positive change in the lives of those we serve, contributing to their personal growth and the broader development of society.
